*A personal year-in-review through data analytics*

## Overview
Career Wrapped 2025 is a personal data analytics project where I turned my year into data to better understand how I worked, learned, and grew across 2025.

Inspired by the idea of yearly wrap-ups (like Spotify Wrapped), this project focuses not on highlights alone, but on **patterns** ‚Äî effort, productivity, stress, burnout, recovery, wins, and losses.

This project uses my own data as a **case study**, but the structure is designed as a **reusable framework** that can be applied to anyone who wants to reflect on their year through data.

---

## Problem Statement
We often remember our year based on outcomes and highlights, but rarely analyze:
- Where our effort actually went  
- When productivity peaked or dropped  
- How stress influenced performance  
- How burnout and recovery unfolded over time  

This project aims to bridge that gap by using data analytics to convert a year of activity into meaningful insights and reflections.

---

## Dataset
The dataset represents key phases across the year rather than daily tracking.

**Key features include:**
- Effort hours (scaled to approximate monthly investment)
- Productivity and stress scores
- Focus areas (skills, projects, preparation)
- Outcomes (wins, losses, neutral phases)
- Key highlights and reflections

> Note: Effort hours are aggregated to reflect realistic yearly investment while preserving relative trends.

---

## Key Insights
- High effort did not always translate to high productivity  
- Burnout followed sustained stress without recovery  
- Breaks helped restore momentum rather than slow progress  
- Hands-on projects led to higher productivity than theory-heavy phases  
- Growth was not linear, but it was consistent over time
